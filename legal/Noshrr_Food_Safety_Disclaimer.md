# Food Safety Disclaimer for tleten

tleten is a platform that connects users who choose to share, exchange, barter, offer, request, donate, or otherwise transfer homemade meals, food items, ingredients, and other approved food-related goods.

**tleten does not prepare, cook, package, inspect, store, transport, deliver, test, certify, endorse, or guarantee any food or item listed on the platform.**

All food and items offered through tleten are provided by independent users, not by tleten.

## Important Food Safety Notice

Items listed on tleten may be:

- homemade
- homegrown
- gathered
- prepared in private homes or other non-commercial environments
- stored or transported by independent users

These environments may **not** be inspected by any government or health authority.

## Risks You Accept

By using tleten, you understand and agree that:

- ingredient and allergen information may be incomplete or inaccurate;
- food may contain allergens or may have been exposed to cross-contact or cross-contamination;
- food may be undercooked, improperly stored, spoiled, contaminated, or otherwise unsafe;
- dietary labels such as “gluten-free,” “vegan,” or similar may reflect only the user’s own understanding;
- food may not be prepared in compliance with local health regulations; and
- consuming food from other users carries inherent risks, including foodborne illness, allergic reaction, choking, injury, or, in rare cases, serious illness or death.

## Your Responsibility

You are solely responsible for:

- reviewing item descriptions carefully;
- asking questions about ingredients, allergens, preparation methods, storage, and freshness;
- deciding whether an item is appropriate for your dietary, allergy, or medical needs;
- properly transporting, refrigerating, reheating, or storing food after pickup or delivery; and
- using your own judgment before accepting or consuming any item.

## No Medical or Dietary Advice

tleten does not provide medical, allergy, nutritional, or dietary advice.

If you have food allergies, food sensitivities, immune compromise, pregnancy-related dietary restrictions, chronic health conditions, or other medical concerns, you should use extreme caution and consult a qualified healthcare professional where appropriate.

## Assumption of Risk

**By using tleten, you voluntarily accept all risks associated with obtaining, handling, transporting, storing, reheating, and consuming any food or item obtained through the platform.**

If you are not comfortable with these risks, do not use tleten to obtain or consume food from other users.
