Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 3/17/2026  |  Last Updated: 3/17/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how tleten ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the tleten mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Account Information

  • name or display name
  • username
  • email address
  • profile photo
  • authentication provider information (such as Sign in with Apple or Google)

b. Profile and Listing Information

  • bio or profile details
  • item listings and descriptions
  • ingredients and allergen information
  • photos uploaded to listings
  • availability or pickup details

c. Messages and User Content

  • messages exchanged through the Service
  • comments, reviews, ratings, and reports
  • other content you upload or submit

d. Device and Usage Information

  • device type
  • operating system
  • app version
  • IP address
  • approximate location (if enabled)
  • crash logs
  • analytics and usage data

e. Transaction Information

If payments are enabled in the future, we may collect or receive:

  • transaction history
  • payment status
  • refund or dispute records

We do not store full payment card information ourselves unless explicitly stated. Payments may be processed by third-party providers.

f. Location Information

If you allow location access, we may collect approximate or precise location information to help show nearby listings, pickups, or local features.

You can disable location permissions in your device settings.

2. How We Use Information

We may use your information to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • authenticate you;
  • display your profile and listings to other users;
  • enable messaging and community interactions;
  • help users find nearby listings;
  • improve safety, moderation, and trust;
  • respond to support requests;
  • detect fraud, abuse, or policy violations;
  • analyze app performance and usage;
  • send service-related notices and updates;
  • enforce our Terms, Community Guidelines, and other policies; and
  • comply with legal obligations.

Certain information is visible to other users as part of the Service, including:

  • your username or display name

  • messages and interactions with users you communicate with

We may share information with vendors and service providers who help us operate the Service, such as:

The Service may use third-party services, including Firebase and related Google services, for:

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the ability to:
